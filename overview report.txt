StudioLAB ek comprehensive dance studio management platform hai. Iske main features ko hum kuch bade sections mein divide kar sakte hain:

1. Unified Management Workspace (Sab kuch ek jagah)
Easy Management: Isme aap apni poori studio business ko bina kisi stress ke ek hi workspace se manage kar sakte hain.

Dashboard: Aapko ek central view milta hai jahan se students, families, enrollments, aur classes ki saari jankari dikhayi deti hai.

2. Student aur Family Portals
Family Portal: Parents ke liye ek alag mobile-friendly portal hota hai jahan wo apne bachon ke classes aur payments dekh sakte hain.

Online Registration: Naye students aur families asani se online register kar sakte hain.

3. Class aur Attendance Tracking
Attendance List: Teachers asani se har class ki attendance mark kar sakte hain.

Scheduling: Classes, events, aur performances ko schedule karna aur unka track rakhna bahut simple hai.

4. Billing aur Payments (Sabse zaruri)
Integrated Billing: Studio ka apna ek billing system hai jisse tuition fees manage hoti hai.

Auto-Pay Options: Isme automatic payment ke kai saare options hain taaki bar-bar invoice bhejne ki mehnat na karni pade.

Flexible Charging: Fees lene ke liye isme bahut flexible solutions diye gaye hain.

5. Communication Hub
Direct Messaging: Aap apne database se sidhe email ya SMS ke zariye baat kar sakte hain.

Instant Updates: Kisi bhi class change ya payment reminder ke liye turant message bheja ja sakta hai.

6. Design aur Experience
Stylish Looks: Iska UI bahut hi modern aur clean hai, jo use karne mein bahut smooth lagta hai.

Multi-Discipline Support: Ye sirf dance ke liye hi nahi, balki gymnastics, cheer, aur drama studios ke liye bhi kaam karta hai.
